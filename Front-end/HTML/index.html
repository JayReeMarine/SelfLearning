Rick-rolling

"Never Gonna Give You Up" is a pop song by English singer Rick Astley, released on 27 July 1987.

The song became a worldwide hit, initially in the United Kingdom in 1987, where it stayed at the top of the chart for five weeks and was the best-selling single of that year.

The video resurged in popularity in 2007 due to the bait-and-switch "Rickroll"Internet meme.
